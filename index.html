About

Vanade (From vanadium, the chemical element with atomic number 23), is a project to make the first XHTML web editor online. The goal is to make a lightweight stable application that helps webdevelopers to make their webpages, always aviable from anywhere, without having to install anything into their computers.

Feel free to collaborate with the proyect. Contact the project developer at buzon@ayotli.com
